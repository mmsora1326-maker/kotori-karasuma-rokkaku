Japanese Culture Experience

At Kotori Karasuma Rokkaku's Japanese cultural experience, you can enjoy four experiences in one session: Calligraphy, tea ceremony, semi-formal kimono, bridal kimono photography. You can not only see but also actually experience Kyoto's unique traditional culture, creating special memories.

Flow of experience

Calligraphy experience

You will experience the culture of Japanese calligraphy using a brush and ink. The completed work will be placed in a hanging scroll case, and you can take it home and enjoy displaying it there.

Tea ceremony experience

While learning about the etiquette and spirit of the tea ceremony, you will prepare your own matcha tea. Enjoy the matcha you have prepared yourself, along with Japanese sweets.

Semi-formal kimono

In a tranquil atmosphere reminiscent of Kyoto, you will wear a semi-formal kimono. While experiencing traditional Japanese attire, you can also enjoy taking photographs.

Bridal kimono photography

The bride will wear a white shiromuku or a colorful uchikake, and experience a truly authentic Japanese wedding photoshoot. The groom will also wear a formal hakama, and enjoy commemorative photos in traditional Japanese wedding attire.

Recommended for these people

  • For those who want to create memories of their trip to Kyoto
  • Overseas visitors who want to experience Japanese culture
  • Those who want to have photos taken of themselves in a kimono
  • For couples or married couples who wish to participate
