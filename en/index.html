About KOTORI

This is a cultural experience salon located in Karasuma Rokkaku, Kyoto. You can casually experience traditional Japanese culture through activities such as calligraphy, tea ceremony, kimono dressing, bridal kimono photography, tsumami zaiku (traditional fabric craft), yukata shopping, and self-dressing lessons.

Experience Details

Japanese Cultural Experience

This is a special cultural experience where you can enjoy calligraphy, tea ceremony, kimono, and bridal kimono photography all at once.

Detail

Tsumami-zaiku workshop

Experience the traditional Japanese craft of "Tsumami-zaiku" (folded fabric flower making). Even beginners can enjoy creating their own piece, and You can take your finished work home with you.

Detail

Yukata shopping & self-dressing

Choose your favorite yukata and learn how to put it on yourself with our dressing lesson. A popular experience you can enjoy even after your trip to Kyoto.
