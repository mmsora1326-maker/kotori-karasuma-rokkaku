Yukata shopping

At Kotori Karasuma Rokkaku, you can purchase your own yukata and experience Japanese summer culture while learning how to wear it. This is a special experience aimed not only at taking home a souvenir of your trip, but also at enabling you to wear it yourself.

Flow of experience

Yukata shopping

Choose your favorite colors and patterns, and select the yukata, obi, and accessories that suit you best. Find your perfect outfit to remember your time in Kyoto.

Self-dressing lesson

Using the yukata you purchased, we will provide a lesson to help you learn how to wear it yourself. From how to put on the yukata to tying the obi, we will guide you clearly and carefully.

Recommended for these people

  • For those who want to buy their own unique yukata
  • Those who want to learn how to wear a yukata
  • For those who want to capture photos that are characteristic of Kyoto
  • For those who want to experience Japanese culture more deeply

Yukata shopping

Please arrive at the front of the kimono shop "YOUSAI" at least 10 minutes before the start of your experience. Our staff will greet you and guide you through everything from choosing your yukata to shopping. We have a wide range of sizes available for both men and women, up to size 5L.
