Tsumami-zaiku experience

Experience "Tsumami-zaiku," a traditional Japanese craft passed down since the Edo period. By folding small pieces of fabric one by one, you can create flowers and seasonal motifs, and appreciate the beauty of delicate Japanese handiwork. You can take your finished work home with you.

Flow of experience

Tsumami-zaiku production

Using specially made fabric, you will learn the basic techniques of tsumami zaiku (traditional Japanese fabric flower making) with care. Even beginners can participate with confidence, as our staff will provide support every step of the way.

Take home your finished artwork

The finished piece can be used as a hair ornament or accessory. Take home a one-of-a-kind piece as a memento of your trip to Kyoto.

Recommended for these people

  • Those interested in traditional Japanese crafts
  • For those who want to experience Kyoto's unique culture
  • People who love making things
  • For those who want to preserve their travel memories in a tangible form.
